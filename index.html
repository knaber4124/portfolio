My name is Keith Naber and thank you for visiting my portfolio page which will give you an idea of who I am, show you a quick simpling of the work I have done during my coding bootcamp experience and allow you to send me feedback on everything you see here.

For most of my professional life I have been in sales and customer service roles. Over the past few years, I have been increasingly looking for a new challenge and way to better the lives of my family. While doing some soul searching one day, I stumbled upon an ad for the UNH Coding Bootcamp and was quite intrigue. In college I had taken a very rudimentary course on HTML,CSS, and overall web design and enjoyed myself quite a bit. After doing some additional research, I decided that this was the exact career change that I was looking for and signed up the bootcamp.

The bootcamp experience is a very challenging and rewarding one. The need to continually reseach and learn is something that I enjoy a great deal as it requires me to continually better and challenge myself. While I enjoy the individual work, the group projects are by far the most rewarding activites that we have done. These projects not only allow you to challenge yourself on more complex problems but also allows you to work closely with other members of the class and get the best work out of everyone.

Personally, I am a very outgoing person who loves to challenge myself and others around me. It is my firm belief that when we combine individual drive and determination with group dynamics and accountability we are able to achieve incredible things. I am enjoy history, politics, most sports and spending time with my family. I hope that you enjoy my portfolio and look forward to hearing your feedback.
